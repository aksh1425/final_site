COMPRESSION STOCKINGS

You wear compression stockings to improve blood flow in your legs. Compression stockings gently squeeze your legs to move blood up your legs. This increase in blood flow can aid or prevent symptoms of pain.

Advantages of compression stockings:

  • Increased blood flow in lower limbs
  • Helping with achy or heavy feeling in the legs
  • Maintaining blood flow during flights or long periods of sitting
  • Prevention of blood clots (primarily after surgery)
  • Decreased swelling
