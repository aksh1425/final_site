TENS7000


Stop your pain dead in its tracks!
Feel instant relief from your chronic pain with this compact, proven device. You’ll enjoy:

  • many hours of pain free living
  • an increase in your daily activity levels
  • pain free body movements
  • higher levels of energy

The TENS7000 is a portable pain management digital device that is easy to operate and compact making it a convenient option to stop your pain. The large LCD screen shows the exact parameters being used.

Includes: device, battery, set of lead wires, set of pads, carrying case (10cm x 6cm x 2.1cm)

Features:

  • Channels: 2 channels (4 electrodes)
  • Wave form: Asymmetrical, Biphasic Square Pulse (balanced)
  • Pulse Amplitude: Adjustable, 80mA max. output
  • Pulse Frequency: Adjustable, from 1 to 150Hz
  • Pulse Width: Adjustable, from 30 to 260uS
  • Power Source: 9V alkaline or rechargable
  • Electrodes: set of 4 pre-gelled reusable electrodes
  • Stimulation modes available: Burst (B), Normal (N), Modulation (M), Modulation (S1), Modulation (S2)
